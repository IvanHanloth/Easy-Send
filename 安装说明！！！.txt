直接访问/install即可安装
注意，需要配置伪静态才能正常使用！

Apache：
RewriteEngine on
RewriteBase /
RewriteRule ^download/?$ /index.php?mode=download [QSA,L]
RewriteRule ^user/?$ /index.php?mode=user [QSA,L]
RewriteRule ^app/?$ /index.php?mode=app [QSA,L]



Nginx（推荐）：
rewrite ^/download+(?:\/?)+(.*)$ /index.php?mode=download&$1;
rewrite ^/app+(\/?)$ /index.php?mode=app;
rewrite ^/user+(\/?)$ /index.php?mode=user;
